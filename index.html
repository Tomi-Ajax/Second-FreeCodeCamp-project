Alexander Pushkin

The father of Russian literature

Pushkin was a Russian poet, playwright, and novelist of the Romantic era. He is considered by many to be the greatest Russian poet and the founder of modern Russian literature

Quick Biography of Alexander Pushkin

  • He was born into the Russian nobility in Moscow on the 6th of June 1799
  • He died in Saint Petersburg, on the 10th of February 1837 age 37
  • His maternal great-grandfather was Major-General Abram Petrovich Gannibal (a Russian black nobleman of Cameroonian origin)
  • He published his first poem at the age of 15.
  • He died from wounds he received in a duel.
